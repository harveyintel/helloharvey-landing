Operations

Harvey Systems operates across archives, documents that persist beyond searchable OCR, incomplete memory, adversarial environments, and compressed investigative timelines.

Methodology prioritizes attributable records, continuity of evidence, and preservation of contradiction where premature resolution would distort factual structure.

Formal discovery rarely arrives as a complete narrative. Harvey Systems works within the production and across public records, regulatory archives, historical materials, to reconstruct operational histories, regulatory continuity, and institutional context.

Context

Harvey Systems developed within the evidentiary demands of Gulf South legacy contamination litigation.

The work is informed by Gulf South realities: intergenerational memory, institutional contradiction, environmental persistence, and the long afterlife of industrial decisions.

Methodology emphasizes accountability to the record, respect for affected communities, and preservation of evidence across time.

Privacy

Harvey Systems operates with a privacy-first methodology. Sensitive communications, investigative materials, and source records are handled with restricted disclosure and minimal-retention practices.
