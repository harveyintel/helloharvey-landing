Litigation Intelligence · Environmental Research · Structured Investigation
Overview

Harvey Systems develops structured investigative and analytical infrastructure for complex litigation, environmental exposure research, and large-scale evidentiary analysis.

The system is designed for high-volume document environments where narrative contradiction, historical fragmentation, and dispersed records require disciplined reconstruction and pattern analysis.

Core Functions
  • Environmental records analysis
  • Exposure reconstruction
  • Forensic document review
  • Evidentiary pattern mapping
  • OSINT-supported investigative research
  • Litigation support infrastructure
  • Structured archival analysis
Operating Principles

Harvey Systems prioritizes evidentiary integrity, structured reasoning, privacy, and analytical restraint.

The system does not provide legal representation, speculate on liability, or generate conclusions unsupported by documentary evidence.
